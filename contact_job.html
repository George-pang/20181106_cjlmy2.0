职位描述

1、 负责中心前台来电课程咨询，提供专业的课程介绍和讨论。
2、 电话预约每周公开体验课程，服务现有客户，保持积极沟通。持续跟进公司潜在客户，提供优质的服务理念，保证公开课预约到访数量与邀访率。
3、 与中心到访家长进行课程规划沟通，根据客户年龄和需求推荐合适课程，安排教学部门对潜在客户进行英语能力评测，安排适龄适级课程。
4、 维护现有客户关系，协助教学部跟踪现有学员学习状况，与家长共同帮助学员熟悉长颈鹿美语教育理念与体系，邀请现有学员参加中心丰富多彩的节庆活动与俱乐部 活动，推荐中心推出新的课程。取得现有客户对中心与课程的及时反馈，保证中心与学员通畅沟通并开发现有客户推荐其他学员。

任职要求

1、 具有在儿童英语教育中心或相似教育机构工作经验。
2、 具备强抗压性，勇于接受销售挑战，完成每月销售目标。
3、 本科及以上学历。
4、 两年以上在高端快速消费行业销售工作经验为佳。
5、 良好的英语能力，出色的沟通能力，声音甜美。

Requirements:

Giraffe English is looking for both part time and full time teachers. The main qualifications for working at Giraffe English are a love of children and a desire to see them grow and learn. Teachers should be patient and willing to develop their teaching skills according to the requirements of the students they teach. We believe that learning a language is also an approach to a culture.

1、Native English speakers with North American accent
2、A four-year bachelor's degree or a master degree from an accredited college or university
3、TEFL, TESL or equivalent certifications
4、Good communication skills and presentation of demo classes, activities...etc.
5、Good command of story telling in English
6、Available to work evening hours and work on weekends
7、One-year of contract with Giraffe English
8、Teaching experience at English centers in Shanghai or in Taiwan would be a plus
9、Initial training and orientation is required before teaching.

Responsibilities：

1、Chinese and foreign teachers have an equal share of the teaching responsibilities
2、Both teachers have equal status and are expected to coordinate their lesson plans and discuss student progress and performance prior to class
3、Chinese teachers have the additional responsibilities of giving and correcting student's homework, giving regular quizzes, giving recording assignments and conducting telephone teaching.
